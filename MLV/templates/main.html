Venn-diaNet, a Venn diagram based analysis framework that uses network propagation upon PPI (protein-protein interaction) network to prioritizes genes from experiments that have multiple DEG lists.
Venn-diaNet is especially useful when researchers tries to:
[1] prioritize genes that are located in the segments in the Venn diagram (especially, intersection).
[2] Evaluate which genes are more impacted by the combination of treatments.
[3] Compare multiple lists of DEGs at once.
